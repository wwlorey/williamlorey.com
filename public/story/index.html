I spent the better part of my time in college working on stuff that didn't make me happy. Set aside the school work, I'm talking extracurriculars. The work I was doing on the S&T Solar Car Team was cool and fast paced, but I struggled to find the value in it. Because of my commitment to that activity, I sacrificed my health, and relationships. I backed out of family vacations and spent less time doing things that made me happy. It wasn't until I took a step back that I realized that working for the sake of work was not the path I wanted to go down. I took time to reconsider what I wanted my life to look like.
I'm lucky enough to have a say in what work I spend my life doing. While my focus may change throughout my life, my current focus is building stuff for the web.
