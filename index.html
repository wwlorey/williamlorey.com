Missouri S&T Solar Car Team

I have been a member of the Missouri S&T Solar Car Team ever since I began attending S&T and it has proven to be very fun and rewarding activity. The team, comprised entirely of students, designs, builds, and races solar powered vehicles in competitions on world stages: most notably the American Solar Challenge and World Solar Challenge.

I am a member of the organization's Electrical sub-team as well as the Logistics Manager. As an Electrical sub-team member, I work on embedded programming projects as well as PCB assembly and design, vehicle wiring, and battery box upkeep. As the team's Logistics Manager, I am the head of the Logistics sub-team which coordinates efforts regarding getting the team, equipment, and car to races, as well as race strategy. Currently the Logistics sub-team is planning for the team's trip to the Bridgestone World Solar Challenge in Australia next Fall.
